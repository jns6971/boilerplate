About

If I had to pick one piece of art that reflects myself, I would chose The Thinker. The reason being that, I am a man of little words but I am always thinking; of what I should build next, what next technology I want to learn next, or how to make the perfect steak. I love being a web developer because it is a mixture of development theory and creativity.

I graduated from RIT in 2013 and moved to Boston, MA where I started my web development career. In 2015 I moved out to Los Angeles, CA where I reside now and still continue developing. I have been working in the Advertising Industry for 3 years now and have experience working for a handful of Ad Agencies; SapientNitro, Arnold Worldwide and Phenomenon. During my time in the advertising industry I stived to fine-tune my professional skills to be well rounded so I can easily step into any new industry, understand their product/service, and create the best website(s) for their needs.

Skills

As a developer, I have learned a wide variety of front-end technologies. I always develop using HTML5 and CSS3 standards. I have experience with vanilla Javascript as well as many different Frameworks and Libraries. Specifically, I have worked with Backbone, jQuery, RequireJS, Underscore, Handlebars, GreenSock, D3, and many, many more. I also have workflow experience with Git, SVN, Gulp, Grunt, JSPM, and Bower.

    Overview
